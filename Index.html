COVID Precautions & Health Practices

My top priority is to ensure a safe and healthy environment for all my collaborations. Here’s how I maintain rigorous health standards:

  • Remote Collaboration: I use video calls and cloud-based tools to create music together from a safe distance.
  • Pre-Session Testing: For necessary in-person meetings, all participants are required to take a rapid COVID PCR test before we start.
  • Strict Mask Protocols: During in-person sessions, both parties wear N95 masks to minimize risk.
  • Sanitization: Equipment and workspaces are thoroughly cleaned and disinfected before and after sessions.
  • Stay Updated: I monitor local health guidelines and adjust my practices to ensure maximum safety.
